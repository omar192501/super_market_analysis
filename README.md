# 📊 Demand, Elasticity & Profit Optimization Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between product price and quantity sold using a real-world retail dataset. The goal is to understand demand behavior, calculate price elasticity, and identify the optimal pricing strategy that maximizes profit.

This project combines concepts from:

* Microeconomics (demand & elasticity)
* Accounting (revenue, cost, profit)
* Quantitative Methods (regression analysis)

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle (Supermarket Sales Dataset)
* Key variables:

  * Unit Price
  * Quantity
  * Total Sales

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies

* Python 🐍
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Scikit-learn

---

## 📊 Key Steps

### 1. Data Preparation

* Sorted dataset by price
* Calculated percentage change using `pct_change()`

### 2. Demand Analysis

* Visualized relationship between price and quantity
* Measured correlation

### 3. Price Elasticity

* Calculated elasticity using percentage change
* Interpreted customer sensitivity to price

### 4. Regression Model

* Built a linear regression model to predict demand
* Analyzed the relationship between price and quantity

### 5. Profit Optimization

* Calculated revenue and cost
* Identified the price that maximizes profit

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Demand shows a negative relationship with price (law of demand)
* Price elasticity indicates that demand is inelastic
* The optimal price for maximum profit is approximately between 90 and 100
* Maximum profit is achieved at a balance between price and sales volume
